## Learned Preferences

### Coaching Delivery

- **Morning message** with the day's plan: what to focus on, main goal of the session, general split overview.
- **Weekly snapshot** with performance summary, trend analysis, any plan adjustments.
- **Tone:** Encouraging but direct. No hedging, no fluff. Say what needs to be said.
- **Detail level:** Daily = concise action plan. Weekly = deeper analysis with numbers.

### Training Preferences

- Mountain days ARE training — never flag them as "missed gym sessions."
- If touring was heavy on the weekend, Monday upper body should auto-adjust intensity downward.
- Prefers compound movements over isolation work.
- Likes variety in core exercises (not just planks).
- Mobility work should feel like a routine, not an afterthought — build it into the weekly structure.
